Impacts of Climate Change

Climate change is already affecting our planet in numerous ways:

Rising Sea Levels: Melting glaciers and ice sheets contribute to rising sea levels, threatening coastal cities and islands.
Extreme Weather Events: More frequent and intense extreme weather events, such as hurricanes, wildfires, and floods, are causing significant damage and loss of life.
Ocean Acidification: Increased carbon dioxide absorption by oceans leads to ocean acidification, harming marine ecosystems.
Biodiversity Loss: Climate change is disrupting ecosystems and leading to species extinction.
Human Health Impacts: Heatwaves, air pollution, and waterborne diseases are increasing health risks.
